TAP THE GHOST

👻 ABOUT

Tap the Ghost is a remake of Zap the Ghost, an Android game I published in 2012 — built with AndEngine and delisted in 2016. A haunted manor, one night that never ends, and a simple rule: tap the bad guys, spare the good ones.

Everything here is hand-rolled: vanilla JavaScript, an inline SVG scene and characters, and every sound effect synthesized live with the Web Audio API. No frameworks, no asset files, no build step.

Design & concept: Flavius Ivasca
Remade & improved in 2026 with AI-assisted coding (Claude Code)

👻 HOW TO PLAY

The night never ends — survive as long as you can and score as many points as possible! Creatures appear in the manor's windows one at a time, and the longer you last, the faster they get.

Tap the BAD guys for +3 points. If one escapes untapped you lose 1 point — and if 3 escape in a row, the ghosts overwhelm you and you lose a life 💔! Watch for the 👻 warnings next to your hearts:

Never tap the GOOD guys — that costs a life 💔. Let them leave safely for +1 point:

Chain bad-guy taps to build a COMBO — ×2 points at a 5 streak, ×3 at 10. Missing a bad guy or tapping a good guy breaks it!

Watch for rare visitors:

Golden ghost — +10 points, but gone in a blink!

Heart fairy — tap her to win back a lost life. She visits at most 3 times a night!

Bomb — do NOT tap, or it costs you a life!

You have 3 lives ❤️. The game ends when you run out. Good luck!
