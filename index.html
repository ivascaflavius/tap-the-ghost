👻 HOW TO PLAY

The goal of this game is to score as many points as possible before the timer runs out — or before you lose all your lives!

Creatures appear in the windows of the haunted manor, one at a time. Tap the BAD guys to earn +3 points:

👻 🧛 🐺 🎃 💀 😈 🧟 🧙

But watch out — GOOD guys live there too. Tap one of them and you lose a life 💔. Let a good guy leave safely and you earn +1 point:

🐶 🐱 🐼 🐰 🦉 🐥

Careful though — if a bad guy escapes untapped, you lose 1 point!

You start with 3 lives ❤️ and a set timer ⏱️ that depends on the difficulty. On higher difficulties creatures appear faster and wait less for you — but the timer is longer, so bigger scores are possible!
